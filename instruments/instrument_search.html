Legend of Mana - Instrument Search

Select the attributes/materials you want in an instrument. Click "Get Results!" and you'll be given a list of all instrument/material/coin combinations that will get you a spell with the attributes you want, with the most powerful results at the top. Mouse over the title of each filter for more details.

Credit to Sevon for an amazing instrument spreadsheet which served as the source for this data.

Charge Time

The amount of time you must hold down the cast button to charge the spell to full range. Charging affects range only, not damage or effect.





Range Shape

The shape of the spell.

Circle = a ring centered on the caster
Donut = a rotating set of 4 lines appear around the caster
Fan = a triangle that spreads out from the caster in the direction they are facing
Line = a horizontal line centered on the caster
Sphere = a sphere centered on the caster
Random = a sphere centered on a random location on the field
Control = a sphere with a position that can be controlled using the analog stick during casting








Trigger Type

Lock holds targets in place during the casting animation. Fire does not.





Coin Type

Would you prefer to use a silver or gold coin during crafting? Gold is more powerful, but also much rarer.



Element

Element of the spell, and type of coin used during crafting.

Wisp = Light
Shade = Dark
Dryad = Nature/Trees
Aura = Gold
Salamander = Fire
Gnome = Earth
Jinn = Air
Undine = Water









Instrument Type

Which instrument type do you want? Note that instrument type has no direct effect on combat - a Fireball spell is a Fireball spell, whether it's being cast from a flute or a drum. However, having a variety of instruments helps make it easier to collect coins.
